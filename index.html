BAGHDAD

Founded in 762 CE by Caliph Al-Mansur, this city became the "City of Peace." Its unique circular design represented the center of the world.

GOLDEN AGE

Baghdad was the global hub for science, medicine, and trade. Scholars traveled thousands of miles to study here.

HOUSE OF WISDOM

At the center stood the Grand Library. It housed the world's knowledge and ancient texts translated into Arabic.
